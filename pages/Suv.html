What is an SUV?

A Sport Utility Vehicle is a car that combines elements of road-going passeger cars with features from off-road vehicles, such as raised ground clearance and four-wheel-drive. However, there is no commonly agreed-upon formal definition of an SUV and usage of the term varies between countries.

Is SUV same as 4x4?

The key difference is the drive type, a 4x4 means it's a four-wheel whereas an SUV can be a 2-wheel drive or have the ability to adapt to a 4-wheel drive.
